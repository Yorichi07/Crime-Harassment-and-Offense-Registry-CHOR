CRIME PREDICTOR

The Crime Predictor utilizes historical crime data spanning the past years in India to forecast the approximate number of crimes for a future year. The system employs a linear regression model, a statistical technique that identifies patterns and relationships in the data, to make predictions. The prediction is based on the assumption that there is a linear correlation between the year and the total number of reported crimes.
